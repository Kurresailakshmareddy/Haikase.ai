Our Service For AI Consumers

Business development: Using clients existing business strategy to create and refine innovative ideas for their business development. This will include developing guidelines to execute and commercialise an idea whilst also, helping our clients to realise cross-sector application opportunities for their ideas.

AI Strategy and Roadmap: Focus on helping clients define their AI strategy, identifying relevant use cases, and creating a roadmap for AI adoption within their organization. This can involve assessing their current state, evaluating potential AI applications, and recommending the most suitable AI technologies and solutions.

AI Vendor Selection and Evaluation: Assist clients in selecting the right AI vendors and technologies for their specific needs. This can involve researching and evaluating various AI platforms, tools, and services available in the market and providing recommendations based on client requirements.

AI Readiness Assessment: Conduct assessments to determine an organization's readiness for AI implementation. This includes evaluating their data infrastructure, data quality, and internal capabilities to leverage AI effectively. Provide recommendations and action plans to bridge any gaps.

AI Use Case Identification and Prioritization: Work closely with clients to identify and prioritize AI use cases that align with their business objectives. This involves understanding their industry, processes, and pain points, and then recommending AI solutions that can address those challenges.

AI Ethics and Governance Consulting: Guide clients in understanding and implementing ethical AI practices. Assist in developing policies, guidelines, and frameworks for responsible AI usage, addressing concerns such as bias, privacy, and transparency. Also, navigating and updating businesses on current and upcoming regulations and policies.

AI Project Management and Oversight: Provide project management support for AI initiatives, ensuring that projects stay on track, meet objectives, and align with client expectations. Offer guidance on project planning, resource allocation, risk management, and progress monitoring.

AI ROI Assessment: Help clients measure their AI initiatives' return on investment (ROI). Develop methodologies to quantify the impact of AI implementations on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as cost savings, revenue growth, customer satisfaction, or operational efficiency.

AI Research and Trends Analysis: Stay up to date with the latest advancements and trends in the field of AI. Provide clients with insights on emerging technologies, industry best practices, and potential opportunities for AI adoption in their specific domain

EU AI Act conformity: Advising clients on the effect of the EU AI Act on their businesses and business strategies as well as supporting them in evaluating already existing AI tools within the company on their conformity once the EU AI Act comes into effect.

Document development: we develop technical newsletters, whitepapers, and technology landscape maps.
